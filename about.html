Our Story

My name is Vashanna Moorer, Owner and Premium Event and Balloon Designer. Planning events was a hobby for years doing parties for family, and friends and even planning my own wedding that has now turned into my dream business. Making people’s dreams and visions come to life, and seeing their smiles and joy means the world to me! We need more joy, happy moments, and celebrations of life in the world, especially during these difficult times! I am honored and grateful to be able to be a part of people’s beautiful moments! .

I started the business originally in 2007, this journey has not come without hardship and I had to close in 2010 after the recession. But the desire to create beautiful events and a full-service event facility in the community I grew up in and to create generational wealth pushed me to reopen the business in 2019. As fate would have it COVID hit and we had to shut down temporarily with the rest of the world. In 2021 with no customers or revenue, I was faced with the decision to close permanently.

I had a dream which I now call “The God Dream” the purpose God has for my life after hearing the phrase at a high school graduation ceremony. It’s an impossible and crazy dream until it happens, nothing is impossible with God! I got a word from God and held onto it because feelings don't succeed TRUTH does. Now here I am in 2022, continuing to work towards my mission and grow!
